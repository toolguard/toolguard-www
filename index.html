Up and running in minutes

Three steps to secure your AI agent's tool access.

1

Install ToolGuard

Single binary, no dependencies. Download and run. Works on macOS, Linux, and Windows.

$ brew install toolguard/tap/toolguard
2

Connect your services

Add your MCP servers and credentials through the dashboard. OAuth flow handles the hard parts.

$ toolguard proxy --db ~/.toolguard/toolguard.db
3

Point your AI at the shim

Configure your AI agent to use the ToolGuard shim as its MCP server. Everything flows through the proxy.

$ toolguard shim --proxy https://...
