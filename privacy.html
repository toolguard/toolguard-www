2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide directly

  • Account information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Billing information: Payment method details. Payment data is processed by Stripe and is not stored on our servers.
  • Support communications: Any information you share when contacting us for support.

2.2 Information we collect automatically

  • Usage data: Log data including which ToolGuard features you use, when you use them, and error information.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: See Section 7 (Cookies).

2.3 Information we do NOT store

ToolGuard acts as a real-time security proxy. The following data passes through our Service but is not stored on our servers:

  • Your API keys, OAuth tokens, or credentials for third-party services (these are held in encrypted, zero-knowledge storage and decrypted only in memory during processing).
  • The content of emails, documents, or other data retrieved from third-party services on your behalf.
  • Individual tool call inputs and outputs processed through the proxy.

We are designed so that we cannot read your credentials or the content of what you retrieve. Our role is to proxy requests securely, not to read or retain your data.

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process payments — bill you for your subscription via Stripe.
  • Communicate with you — send transactional emails (receipts, security alerts, service notices) and respond to support requests.
  • Improve the Service — understand how ToolGuard is used (in aggregate, anonymized) to fix bugs and build new features.
  • Comply with legal obligations — retain records as required by law, respond to lawful requests.
  • Prevent fraud and abuse — detect and prevent unauthorized access or policy violations.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising.
