Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

Introduction

At ToolGuard, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our AI security proxy service.

ToolGuard is designed to enhance security by acting as a proxy between AI agents and your tools. We've built our service with privacy-by-design principles to minimize data collection while maximizing security.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address and name when you create an account
  • Organization information for team plans
  • Billing information processed by our payment providers

Usage Data

  • API requests made through ToolGuard (metadata only, not content)
  • Policy configurations and audit logs
  • Performance and error logs for service improvement
  • Authentication tokens (encrypted and never stored in plaintext)

Technical Information

  • IP addresses and device information
  • Browser type and version for dashboard access
  • Cookies for authentication and preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our security proxy service
  • Authenticate API requests and enforce security policies
  • Generate audit logs and security reports
  • Detect and prevent security threats or anomalies
  • Communicate with you about your account and service updates
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Security

Security is our core business, and we apply the same standards to protecting your data:

  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard methods
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access with multi-factor authentication required
  • Credential Isolation: Your API keys and tokens are encrypted and isolated from our systems
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing
  • Zero-Trust Architecture: No implicit trust, all access verified

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • With your consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing
  • Service providers: Trusted third parties who help us operate our service (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal compliance: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale (with notice)

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Account data: Until you delete your account
  • Audit logs: 2 years for security and compliance purposes
  • Usage analytics: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained longer
  • Billing records: As required by law and accounting practices

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below.

International Data Transfers

ToolGuard operates globally, and your data may be processed in countries other than where you reside. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including:

  • Standard contractual clauses approved by relevant authorities
  • Adequacy decisions where available
  • Additional security measures as needed

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Authentication and session management
  • User preferences and settings
  • Analytics to improve our service

You can control cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending an email notification to registered users
  • Providing notice through our service dashboard

Your continued use of ToolGuard after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
