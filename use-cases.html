What ToolGuard does today

How teams use ToolGuard

Real use cases — securing AI agent access to Gmail, managing team credentials, staying compliant, and more.

📬

Secure Gmail Access for AI Agents

Give Claude, Cursor, or any MCP-compatible AI access to Gmail without sharing credentials. ToolGuard holds the OAuth token, the AI gets tool calls, and every email read or sent appears in the audit log. Revoke anytime — without touching your Google account.

👥

Team AI Access Management

Run one gateway for your whole team. Configure services once, and every developer's AI client gets the same tools. Centralized audit log, one place to revoke access — no more API keys scattered across laptops.

🏛️

Compliance-Safe AI Deployment

Self-host on your own infrastructure. Credentials never leave your network. The full audit trail gives compliance teams what they need for review. Works with legal, finance, and healthcare teams where data residency and access logging aren't optional.

🔌

MCP Server Consolidation

Multiple MCP servers across multiple AI clients? ToolGuard is the single connector. Configure once, use from Claude Desktop, Cursor, Continue, or any MCP client. One place to manage credentials, policies, and logs — instead of juggling configs everywhere.

🕐

Contractor & Agent Temporary Access

Provision a scoped token for a contractor or an automated agent, monitor exactly what it does, and revoke cleanly when the engagement ends. No credential sharing. No awkward "can you change the password" conversations after the fact.
